Target Audience:

The target audience are indiviuals who:

  • Are age 12 or older who have access to the internet
  • Have a house income of of about $52,225
  • Are interested in finding accurate readings of the weather in either the surrounding area and/or in their own.
  • Are interested in daily articles about their city and/or those nearby

Personas:

Jessie

Jessie is 31 year old and has a bachelors degree in Education. Ahe is married and currently works as a stay at home mom to 2 kids, one of which is attending Oakwood elementary school. Despite being home most the time Jessie is very social with her neighbors and with the other parents that she sees at the elementary school drop off and pick up. Jessie mostly uses her ipad and phone to search the internet, keep up to date with information about her childs school, and to stay in touch with family out in other towns. Jessie is great at planning events and is often the person in her family that puts together family get togethers and reunions.

Quote: "An educated person becomes a better person and an educated parent creates better people."

Roger

Roger is a 71 year old retired widower who lives alone except for his dog. Aside from taking his dog on walks, he likes to occassionally go fishing at Bear Lake. Roger has a couple of long time friends in the neighborhood as well as some children and grandchildren in the area. Roger keeps to himself most the time and is a somewhat quiet individual, but he does like small talk and visits from family and friends. Aside from the times his children and grandchildren visit, Roger does not have much help navigating media devices and tends to stick to navigating the internet with his trusty desktop computer.

Quote: "You can't live life without enjoying the peace and quiet once in a while"
