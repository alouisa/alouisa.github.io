Weather Site Audience

Target Audience:

Preston, Idaho of Franklin County

Preston has a population of about 5,204 people and are known for their "Napolean Dynamite" Festival (it was created there), the Fraklin County parade (includes local business advertisement and floats), and the "Idaho Festival of Lights" (after Thanksgiving to the end of December). The children of this area attend schools in the Preston School District and a popular recreation area is the nearby Cub River Canyon.

More than half of the population is married and a little less than half of the population have children under 18.

Soda Springs, Idaho of Caibou County

Soda Springs is part of Caribou County and has a population of about 3,058 people and is named "Soda Springs" due to the natural springs of carbonated waters in and around the city. It is also known for the Springs Geyser that is set loose every hour.

Weather here as well as the rest of Idaho is dry year round. It is mostly warm and clear skies in the summer and partly cloudy and freezing in the winter.
