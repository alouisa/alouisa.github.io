Tours

Cozumel has a tropical savanna climate that is believed to have been first settled by the Maya people by the early part of the 1st millenniun AD. Preclassic Olmec artifacts have been found on the island. The largest remaining ruins are at San Gervasio. The name Cozumel was actually given to it by the Spainards but its origin comes from the Mayan words "Cuzam" and "Lumil" which together is translated as "Land of the Swallows." Every year around April and May swallows can be seens in great numbers flying overhead.
